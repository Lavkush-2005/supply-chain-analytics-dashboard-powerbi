# Supply Chain & Logistics Dashboard (Power BI)
## Overview

This project showcases an interactive Supply Chain & Logistics Dashboard of SAMSUNG developed using Power BI.
It provides a comprehensive view of supplier performance, inventory levels, shipment operations, and customer delivery metrics.

The objective of this dashboard is to analyze and visualize supply chain data using Power BI in order to identify inefficiencies, reduce delays, optimize inventory, and improve overall business performance.

# Tools & Technologies
- Power BI
- Microsoft Excel
- Data Modeling
- DAX 

## Key metrices
- Total Revenue: 176.95M  
- Profit: 48.56M  
- Profit Margin: 27.44%  
- Total Shipments: 8K  
- Perfect Order Rate: 75%  

# Key Insights
🔹 **Supplier**   

- **Average lead time** across suppliers is around 11.53 days  
- Countries like India (~9.5 days) and Vietnam (~9.4 days) have the lowest lead times  
- Suppliers like Samsung India & Samsung Vietnam are faster compared to others  

👉 Shifting procurement towards low lead-time regions can significantly improve supply chain efficiency


🔹 **Cost & Order Trends**  

- **Total unit cost** reached 78.13M with 129K orders  
- **Monthly trend shows fluctuations**, with peaks around October–December  

👉 Demand is seasonal — better planning is required during peak months to avoid shortages


🔹 **Quality Analysis**

- **Average supplier quality score** is very high (~96.63)
- Top suppliers like Taiwan Semiconductor & Sony Semiconductor score ~98

👉 Overall supplier quality is strong, but slight improvements can enhance product reliability further


🔹 **Inventory & Production**

- **Total inventory value:** 160K
- Safety stock maintained at 89K
- **Total production:** 4M units
- **Defect rate** is very low (0.24%)

👉 Strong quality control (low defects) However, high safety stock may indicate overstocking


🔹 **Defect Analysis**
- Highest defects seen in:
- Galaxy S24 Ultra (~4.3K)
- Galaxy Buds (~3.4K)

👉 High-demand products also show higher defects → need stricter quality checks


🔹 **Shipment** 
- **Total shipment cost:** 19.42M
- **Total shipments:** 8K
- **On-time delivery rate:** 75%
- **Average delivery time:** 14.19 days

👉 25% shipments are delayed → logistics optimization needed


🔹 **Delay Reasons**
- Carrier capacity issues (highest)
- Documentation issues
- Port congestion

👉 Operational inefficiencies are the main bottleneck, not just logistics


🔹 **Shipment Status**
- ~75% shipments delivered successfully
- Remaining in transit, delayed, or processing

👉 Delivery success rate is decent but can be improved


🔹 **Customer & Revenue Insights**
- **Total revenue:** 176.95M
- Profit: 48.56M
- **Profit margin:** 27.44%

👉 Business is highly profitable with strong margins
